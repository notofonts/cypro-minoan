Noto Sans Cypro Minoan is a design for the historical European Cypro-Minoan script.

Noto Sans Cypro Minoan contains 104 glyphs, and supports 103 characters from the Unicode block Cypro-Minoan.

Supported writing systems

Cypro-Minoan

Cypro-Minoan is a historical European logo-syllabary. Undeciphered syllabary used on the island of Cyprus during the late Bronze Age (1500-1200 BCE) for the Eteocretan language. Read more on ScriptSource, Unicode, Wikipedia, r12a.
